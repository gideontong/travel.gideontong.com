Points Value & Redemptions

The Baseline Value

IHG points are generally valued between 0.5 and 0.66 cents per point. This means a stash of 10,000 points is typically worth about $50 to $66 when applied toward free nights.

Award Pricing Tiers

  • Minimum: 5,000 - 10,000 pts (Budget brands, off-peak)
  • Average: ~60,000 pts (Standard redemptions)
  • Premium: 70,000 - 120,000 pts (InterContinental, Six Senses, Regent)
  • Peak Ultra-Luxury: Up to 200,000 pts

Traveler Card Strategy

The Chase 5/24 Optimization

The IHG Traveler card features no annual fee and historical welcome bonuses peaking at 125,000 points. However, applying directly uses a highly valuable Chase 5/24 slot.

The Optimal Route (Downgrade Strategy)

Apply for the $99-fee IHG Premier card when its bonus peaks (up to 185,000 pts or multiple free nights). Hold it for one year to capture the massive bonus, then call Chase to downgrade to the no-fee Traveler card.

The "4th Night Free" Rule

The strongest reason to hold this card is the 4th Reward Night Free benefit. If you are redeeming points for a stay of 4+ nights, holding the card gives you a mathematical 25% discount on your booking.
