5/24 rule, eligibility & strategy (click to expand) more

Chase 5/24: Strict rule — you cannot be approved if you’ve opened 5+ personal cards (any bank) in the past 24 months. This is the #1 hurdle.

Welcome bonus restriction: Not eligible if you currently hold any personal IHG card or have earned an IHG bonus in the last 24 months.

Traveler vs Premier: Traveler ($0) is a great “set & forget” card, but many experts recommend the Premier ($99) first because its annual free night certificate usually far exceeds the fee. The Traveler card is often used as a downgrade option after year 1 of the Premier.
