🎯 Strategic advice: If you've never held the IHG Premier card and can use the annual free night (value $99+), get the Premier first. Its bonus is much larger (often 140K–175K pts + free night), and the free night alone usually outweighs the annual fee. Add the Traveler card later as a companion no-fee card.

When to skip for now: If you're under 5/24 and still have high-priority Chase cards to acquire (Sapphire Preferred, Southwest, etc.), use those slots first. The Traveler card is a great later addition.
